Our Story

Baked in Guwahati,
made with heart

Why we started cutting cakes

Cut The Damn Cake was born from a simple frustration — the lack of truly great, honest bakeries in Guwahati. We wanted a place where quality came first. Where every cake was made fresh. Where you didn't have to compromise.

We started small — just a home kitchen, a few test batches, and an Instagram page. Word spread fast. The cakes did the talking. Today, we serve hundreds of happy customers every month, still with the same obsessive care for quality.

We're not just a bakery. We're a little bit of joy, delivered to your door.

What drives us

Fresh Ingredients
We use no artificial preservatives. Every cake is baked to order, using the finest quality ingredients we can source.
Made with Love
Every item is handcrafted. We don't do assembly lines — we do meticulous, patient baking the way it should be done.
Always Improving
Our recipes are constantly evolving. Customer feedback shapes everything we do. The best cake is always the next one.
Inclusive Baking
Eggless options, gluten-free choices, pet-safe cakes — we believe everyone deserves a slice of something wonderful.
